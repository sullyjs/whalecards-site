RoadMap

Phase One

We are working hard on finishing the site & whitepaper, and recruiting more team members. Furthermore, we are going to start promotion on the NFTS, open our social media platforms and start development on the $WCS token. A good chunk of NFTS will already be available to be bought on opensea, for early birds. These early birds will also receive a bonus reward on launch. Giveaways will also be organized, which will include NFTs and crypto!

Phase Two

Token will be ready for launch on BSC, including a possible presale and airdrops for excited participants. We aim to get $WCS on the tip.cc bot at this time. Challenges for buying NFTs will be released as soon as the token is ready to go, which early birds will have a bigger chance on completing. Early birds will also receive their bonuses and all leftover NFTs will be released on Opensea. After all the NFTs are released, new NFTs will be provided every week!

Phase Three

A lottery will be started, which will take place weekly. Users can put $WCS tokens in a pool to buy tickets and have a chance on the entire pool! Specifics will be written in the Whitepaper. A plan to burn a percentage of the supply is made. More about this phase coming soon!
