RoadMap

Phase One

The Whalecards team is working hard to finish our official website. In addition to our website, we are working endlessly on our whitepaper which will give you all the information needed about our project. Furthermore are we recruiting more team members to join us to help us develop our very own token, help us create more NFT's, and overall improve our project and reach our goals. Also, with the release of our site will there already be a wide range of NFT's already purchasable on our opensea, for our early birds. Becoming an early bird comes with some perks: as rewards at the launch of our token. Also look out for occasional giveaways, which include NFT's and crypto!

Phase Two

Token will be ready for launch on BSC, including a possible presale and airdrops for excited participants. We aim to get $WCS on the tip.cc bot at this time. Challenges for buying NFT's will be released as soon as the token is ready to go, which early birds will have a bigger chance on completing. Early birds will also receive their bonuses and all leftover NFT's will be released on Opensea. After all the NFT's are released, new NFT's will be provided every week!

Phase Three

A lottery will be started, which will take place weekly. Users can put $WCS tokens in a pool to buy tickets and have a chance on the entire pool! Specifics will be written in the Whitepaper. A plan to burn a percentage of the supply is made. More about this phase coming soon!
