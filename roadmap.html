RoadMap

Phase One

The Whalecards team is working hard to finish our official website. In addition to our website, we are working tirelessly on our whitepaper which will provide all of the information about our upcoming project. Furthermore, we are recruiting more team members to help us develop our very own token, create more NFT's, improve our project, and reach our end goals. Along with the release of our website, there will be a wide range of NFT's available for purchase on our OpenSea page for the early collectors out there. Early collectors will receive special, one-time rewards at the launch of the token for being amazing supporters. Be sure to look out for occasional giveaways that include NFT and crypto-based prizes!

Phase Two

Our token will be ready for launch on the Binance Smart Chain, as well as including a possible pre-sale and airdrops for eager participants. We aim to get $WCS on the tip.cc bot at this time. After the token has officially launched we will release Series 1 of the Collector's Challenges. If you're an early collector you will have a higher chance of completing these. Rules, conditions, and prizes will be announced before we post the challenges. Early collectors will receive their bonuses and all initial NFT's will be released on our OpenSea page. If all of the initial NFT's are sold out before the start of the Series 1 Collector's Challenges, rest assured that there will be a new set of NFT's provided every week!

Phase Three

Once per week we will host a community-based lottery. Community members and collectors alike can use $WCS tokens to buy lottery tickets, giving participants a chance to win 90% of the total tokens spent on tickets for that week's lottery! The remaining 10% will be split into two 5% halves. The first half will be sent to a burn wallet and the second half will be sent to a developer wallet where the accumulated tokens will be utilized for possible expenses, airdrops, etc. Specifics regarding the lottery rules, conditions, and token prize distribution will be documented in the whitepaper. There will also be a plan to burn a percentage of the token supply. More information about this phase will be coming soon!
